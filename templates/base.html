About GCIMB

Chapman University California, Howard University Washington and National Institute of Technology Warangal are organizing its first Global Conference on Innovations in Management and Business during July 27th and 28th, 2021. The conference aims to bring together academia, researchers, and scholars to exchange information and share experiences and research results about all aspects of specialized and interdisciplinary fields.
