Evaluation Process

All submissions will undergo a double-blind peer review. The abstracts will be evaluated by select reviewers identified by the organizing team. If the abstract is accepted, the contributor will be invited by email to submit the full paper including tables, figures, and references. The detailed guidelines for submitting the full paper will be shared in the email.


The authors of accepted abstracts are encouraged to submit the full paper, but it is optional.


If the abstract is accepted, then at least one author must register for the conference to make the presentation. The organizing team will email the guidelines for presentation.


All submissions should report original and previously unpublished research ideas and results.
