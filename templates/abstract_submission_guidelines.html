Abstract Submission Guidelines

Format Guidelines :

Interested contributors are requested to submit an extended abstract of up to 500 words. The abstract should contain the following:

  1. Title of the article and author details
  2. Purpose
  3. Research Methodology/Design
  4. Major Findings
  5. Research Implications
  6. Keywords


Please use Times New Roman with 12 size font.


The contributors must ensure that their submissions are original and have not been presented or published earlier. The submissions should not be concurrently under review for presentation or publication at another research outlet.


The contributions that do not adhere to the above guidelines will not be considered for presentation or publication.


Note : It is not mandatory to submit full papers after your abstracts are accepted. Upon receiving acceptance notification of your abstract, you may directly present your work as per the guidelines the organising team will share. Contributors interested in exploring publication opportunities are encouraged to submit fully developed manuscripts upon acceptance of their abstract. Upon review, all accepted papers will be published in an indexed volume of proceedings by a reputed publisher, such as Springer, Sage, etc.
